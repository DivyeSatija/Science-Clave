The B. Sc. Programme Society

St. Stephen's College

The B.Sc. Programme Society, St. Stephen's College is a student-run society that is formulated on the idea of the importance of multi-disciplinary knowledge in today's world. The society aims at being a platform for all students irrespective of their courses to learn the things that are happening around us. It focusses on organizing a wide array of events like talks, seminars, webinars, workshops, discussions, quizzes along with society's Annual fest Fusion.
